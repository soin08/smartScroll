smartScroll demo: nice scroller

A rotation plugin is used in this demo.
Try the following to see how it works:
  • Scroll down to see the button;
  • Press the button to scroll up, press again to scroll down to the previous position;
  • Press the button to scroll up, than manually scroll down to the previous position or lower;
  • Scroll up and down manually to see the button's behaviour;
  • Scroll down and refresh the page.
